# Thematic Evaluation Template

This template provides a structure for designing a thematic evaluation focusing on a specific topic (e.g., gender inclusion, participation, youth engagement, local capacity or governance).

---

## 1. Theme and Evaluation Questions
Define the theme and frame the questions.

Example (Simulated):
- How effectively were gender considerations integrated into activities?
- What supported or hindered inclusive participation?

---

## 2. Methods
Describe data sources and tools.

Examples:
- Document review  
- Key informant interviews  
- Focus group discussions  
- Indicator analysis  
- Observation checklists  

---

## 3. Findings
Present evidence organized by evaluation question.

Example:
- Training materials incorporated inclusive language.
- Participation varied across regions due to contextual factors.
- Women reported increased confidence but limited time availability.

---

## 4. Learning
Highlight actionable insights.

Example:
- Scheduling of activities should consider caretaking responsibilities.
- Local partners benefit from tailored gender-support resources.

---

## 5. Recommendations
Provide practical and prioritized recommendations.

Example:
- Co-develop inclusion strategies with local partners.
- Strengthen qualitative monitoring on participation quality.
- Use targeted communication strategies for underrepresented groups.

---

This structure can be adapted for any thematic area relevant to MEL.
