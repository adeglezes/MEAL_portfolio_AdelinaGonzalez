# Case Study Example (Simulated)

## Title
Adoption of Climate-Resilient Practices in Country B

## Purpose
To document how and why adoption increased among participants in one region.

---

## Methods
- Focus group discussions with participants.  
- Individual interviews with local partners.  
- Review of monitoring and training records.  
- Simple comparison of baseline and endline indicator values.  

---

## Context
The region experienced variable rainfall and limited access to extension services.  
The program introduced improved practices and follow-up coaching.

---

## Key Findings
- Participants reported higher confidence in applying new practices.  
- Local partners observed stronger peer-to-peer knowledge sharing.  
- Monitoring data showed an increase in adoption from 22% to 48%.  
- Women participants emphasized increased inclusion in decision-making.

---

## Illustrative Quotes (Simulated)
- “We now compare notes every week to see what is working.”
- “The follow-up visit helped us make adjustments.”

---

## Lessons
- Regular coaching supported early adoption.  
- Peer learning groups strengthened motivation.  
- Differences across villages highlight the role of local facilitation.

---

## Recommendations
- Continue structured peer group activities.  
- Provide refresher sessions tailored to local challenges.
