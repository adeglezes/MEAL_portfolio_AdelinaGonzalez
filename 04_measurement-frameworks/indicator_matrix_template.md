# Indicator Matrix Template

This is an example of a basic indicator matrix often used in MEL frameworks.  
It can be adapted to program-specific indicators.

Indicator | Definition | Method | Frequency | Data Source | Responsible
----------|------------|--------|-----------|-------------|------------
Yield change | % change in average yield between baseline and endline | Survey sample | Annual | Survey data | MEL team
Training reach | Number of individuals trained | Administrative count | Quarterly | Training registers | Implementers
Adoption rate | % of participants applying at least one new practice | Survey + observation | Annual | Survey and field visits | MEL team
Learning events | Number of structured learning or reflection sessions | Administrative count | Semi-annual | Event records | Coordination team
Knowledge score | Composite score from training assessment | Short test | Pre/Post | Test results | Trainers
Access to resources | % of participants with access to key inputs | Survey | Annual | Survey | MEL team
