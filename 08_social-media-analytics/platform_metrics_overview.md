# Platform Metrics Overview

The following metrics are commonly extracted from tools such as Metricool, Meta Insights or native platform dashboards. They can support MEL work related to:

- Behavioural trends
- Communication reach
- Digital engagement patterns
- Audience segmentation
- Learning dissemination

## Key Metrics

### 1. Reach
Number of unique users who saw the content.  
Useful for assessing dissemination.

### 2. Impressions
Total times a post was displayed.  
Useful for volume analysis.

### 3. Engagement
Sum of likes, comments, saves, shares and reactions.  
Useful for understanding interaction levels.

### 4. Engagement Rate
Engagement relative to reach or impressions.  
Useful for comparing posts or platforms.

### 5. Follower Growth
Indicates community expansion over time.

### 6. Click-throughs
Useful for calls-to-action or learning content.

### 7. Content Type Breakdown
Photos, videos, stories, reels, carousels.  
Supports understanding of what formats work best.

These metrics are often combined into short monthly or quarterly learning summaries.
