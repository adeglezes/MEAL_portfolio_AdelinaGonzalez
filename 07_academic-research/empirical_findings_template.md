# Empirical Findings Template

This template provides a structure for presenting academic or evaluation findings in a clear and rigorous format.

---

## 1. Research Questions
State the questions guiding the analysis.

## 2. Data Sources
Describe data types, collection methods and sample.

## 3. Descriptive Results
Summaries, tables or brief plots showing distributions, group differences or trends.

## 4. Qualitative Themes
Key themes supported by quotes or examples.

## 5. Inferential Results
Regression, correlations or comparative tests, where appropriate.  
Present coefficients, confidence intervals and brief interpretation.

## 6. Integrated Interpretation
Combine quantitative and qualitative insights to address the research questions.

## 7. Limitations
Describe sample, methodological or contextual limitations.

## 8. Implications
Highlight what findings mean for policy, practice or MEL.
