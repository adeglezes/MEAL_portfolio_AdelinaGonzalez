# Literature Review Synthesis Template

This template provides a structure for summarizing and synthesizing academic literature.

---

## 1. Conceptual Focus
Define the concept or phenomenon of interest.

Example (simulated):
"Determinants of behavioural adoption in development contexts."

## 2. Key Themes in the Literature
- Knowledge, confidence and attitudes.  
- Institutional enablers and constraints.  
- Peer dynamics and social capital.  
- Access to resources and information systems.

## 3. Gaps or Tensions
- Limited comparative research across contexts.  
- Underrepresentation of qualitative evidence.  
- Limited attention to gendered or social dynamics.  
- Mixed findings on the role of follow-up support.

## 4. Relevance to MEL
- Informs indicator selection.  
- Supports validity arguments for scales.  
- Strengthens ToC assumptions.  
- Guides evaluation questions.

## 5. Summary
A short synthesis linking literature to the present research or evaluation.
