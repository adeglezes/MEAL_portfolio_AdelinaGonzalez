# Research–MEL Bridge Note

Academic research and MEL practice share methodological foundations.  
This note summarizes how research competencies support MEL roles.

---

## 1. Construct Clarity
Academic research requires clear definition of constructs and concepts.  
This supports MEL through improved:

- Indicator design  
- Theory of Change articulation  
- Survey and tool development  

## 2. Measurement Quality
Knowledge of reliability and validity strengthens:

- Scale construction  
- Indicator interpretation  
- Quality assurance for monitoring data  

## 3. Analytical Rigor
Statistical reasoning and qualitative analysis improve:

- Evidence interpretation  
- Triangulation  
- Evaluation design  

## 4. Communication of Findings
Academic writing skills enhance:

- Donor reporting  
- Synthesis documents  
- Learning briefs  

## 5. Ethical Practice
Academic standards reinforce MEL principles:

- Data privacy  
- Safe data collection  
- “Do no harm” 

---

Research foundations are an asset in MEL, improving both technical quality and learning opportunities across programs.
