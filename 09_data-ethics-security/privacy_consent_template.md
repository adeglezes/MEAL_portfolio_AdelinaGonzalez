# Privacy and Consent Template (Simulated)

This template outlines basic privacy and consent language for qualitative or survey-based MEL activities.

---

## Introduction
You are invited to share your experiences related to the program. Participation is voluntary.

## Purpose of Data Collection
The information you provide will help improve program design and implementation.

## Confidentiality
- Your name and identifying details will not be included in reports.
- Only authorised team members will have access to the data.
- Data will be stored securely and deleted when no longer needed.

## Risks and Benefits
There are no direct risks expected. You may choose not to answer any question.

## Consent
By participating, you agree that your responses may be analysed anonymously.

This template can be adapted to the requirements of each project.
