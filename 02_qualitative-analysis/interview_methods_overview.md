# Interview Methods Overview

This document outlines qualitative interview procedures used in evaluation and research contexts.

## Design and Sampling
- Semi-structured interviews based on a question guide aligned to the MEL framework.  
- Purposeful sampling of participants, partners and staff familiar with implementation.  
- Flexibility to adapt the guide to respondent expertise and context.  

## Data Collection
- Informed consent obtained verbally or in writing.  
- Interviews conducted in-person or remotely depending on accessibility.  
- Field notes prepared after each interview.  

## Data Handling
- Transcripts stored securely and anonymized.  
- Researcher memos used to document emerging patterns and reflexive observations.  

## Analysis
- Iterative coding using initial, focused and thematic stages.  
- Triangulation with monitoring data and documents.  
- Analytic summaries prepared to link evidence to evaluation questions.
