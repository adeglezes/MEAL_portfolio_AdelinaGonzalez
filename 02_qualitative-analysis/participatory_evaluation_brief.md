# Participatory Evaluation Brief

Participatory approaches help integrate local perspectives and strengthen learning.

## Elements of a Participatory MEL Approach
- Joint definition of learning questions with partners and participants.  
- Co-creation or revision of the Theory of Change.  
- Outcome harvesting to identify unexpected or user-driven changes.  
- Reflection sessions to jointly interpret data and identify learning.  
- Transparent documentation of agreements and next steps.  

## Typical Outputs
- A shared interpretation of changes observed.  
- Actionable learning points for adaptation.  
- Increased stakeholder ownership of evidence.
